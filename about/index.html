The Green Box

Why we built this.

There was a green electrical box on the corner of our block on Staten Island. It was a utility box — the kind you see on every street in every neighborhood in America. Nothing special about it except that everyone knew what it meant.

If you were looking for us, you knew where to go. Show up at the green box and the plan figured itself out. No phones needed. No group chats. Just show up and someone would be there.

That box is where we figured out a lot of things growing up.

Matt and Charlie have been friends since they were five years old. Born in 1984, raised on Staten Island, still figuring things out together forty years later.

Matt is now down in Manahawkin, New Jersey. Charlie is still on Staten Island. The distance has not changed much — we still show up when it counts.

We started Green Box Agency because we kept watching local businesses get burned. Bad websites that cost too much. SEO promises that went nowhere. Agencies that disappeared after the check cleared.

We know these business owners. We grew up around them. The guy who runs the deli. The woman who owns the salon. The contractor who showed up for everyone on the block when something needed fixing.

These people deserve better than what most agencies offer. So we built Green Box to give it to them.

Our Values

How we actually run this thing.

This is not a manifesto. It is how we treat clients on a regular Tuesday.

Show Up

We answer our phones. We respond to emails. We do not disappear. That is not a differentiator — it is the minimum. But you would be surprised how rare it is.

Straight Talk

We tell you what you need, not what sounds impressive. If something is not working we say so. If you do not need a service we will tell you that too.

Neighborhood First

We are not trying to be the biggest agency in New York. We are trying to be the best one for Staten Island businesses that need a real partner.

Built to Last

We measure success in years not months. We want clients who grow with us and refer their friends. That is how the green box always worked — you showed up, you were reliable, and people knew where to find you.
